# Writing Style

- Use clear explanations, simple words, and simple sentences.
- Write for people at all levels of understanding. If a point can be explained like ELI5 without losing accuracy, do that.
- Avoid academic, abstract, or overly polished language.
- Prefer direct phrasing over clever phrasing.
- Keep the author's voice plainspoken, practical, and readable.
- When editing posts, check for wordy sentences, jargon, and phrases that sound more like an essay than a person explaining something clearly.
